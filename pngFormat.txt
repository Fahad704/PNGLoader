8 byte header: 89 50 4E 47 0D 0A 1A 0A

Series of Chunks

Chunk:
	4 byte length
	4 byte type/name
	length byte data
	4 byte CRC checksum(network-byte-order CRC-32)
Critical chunks:
IHDR : 13 bytes data:
	4 byte width
	4 byte height
	1 byte bits per channel
	1 byte color type
	1 byte compression method
	1 byte filter method
	1 byte interlace method

PLTE : Pallete information for indexed file
